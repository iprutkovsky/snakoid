Control/Rules
    Control
  • Begin/continue exploring press Start/Pause button
  • Make a rest press Esc
  • Control Snakoid:
    W ‐ Up, S ‐ Down, A ‐ Left, D ‐ Right,
    Rules
  • Before you start the exploration of the droid world
  • Choose the Canvas Size & Difficulty
  • During the exploration, you will pick up the fruits
  • Sweet fruits will fill up the energy block of Snakoid,
    and new blocks will appear, but the Sour ones will exhaust it,
    and blocks will disappear
  • Your exploration will be stopped if:
    ◦ the quantity of energy blocks is less than three
    ◦ the leading block will come into contact with one of the other blocks
    ◦ the score is less than zero
  • Be sensible so that you can make the right choice
